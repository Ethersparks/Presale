Battle, Train, and Trade Your CryptoSparks

EtherSparks is the world’s first blockchain-linked game. Unlike games which are entirely based on the blockchain, EtherSparks allows players to play single player and multiplayer games in real time without waiting for blockchain confirmations while still preserving ownership of game assets on the blockchain. Unlike purely blockchain based games, which are limited by the delays and costs associated with the Ethereum network, playing EtherSparks does not require repeatedly accessing the network and therefore improves playability at lower fees. This is all done while preserving the main advantage of blockchain games: player ownership of both ERC-721 and ERC-20 compatible game assets. The results of games played as well as any rewards won can all be recorded on the blockchain. By combining seamless gameplay with the blockchain, EtherSparks represents the next generation in Ethereum gaming.

SEAMLESS GAMEPLAY

Play games across multiple genres in real time using assets you own on the blockchain. Items in EtherSparks aren’t just collectibles, they are used to give you an edge in games, both single player and multiplayer.

BLOCKCHAIN SECURE

All items and characters in EtherSparks are stored on the Ethereum network. Once in your wallet, game objects are fully secured by the blockchain. Nobody, not even the developers, can take items in your collection without your permission.

PLAYER DRIVEN UNIVERSE

The world of EtherSparks is both collaborative and competitive. Whether it’s joining together to complete special quests or fighting for the top spot in a tournament, player interaction will govern the outcomes of major events. Similarly, the economy of EtherSparks will be entirely player driven. Everything from the most common commodity to the rarest items will have their price determined by the marketplace.

ETH PRIZES

Part of all earnings collected in the game are returned to players in the form of rewards. Every mini-game has the potential to randomly award the player ETH. In addition, core nodes which entitle owners to a percent of income generated in EtherSparks are awarded in special events and tournaments.

DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP

Presale

April 27, 2018

During the pre-sale, early backers will have the chance to obtain unique loot boxes. These objects will have a chance to contain special pre-sale only objects as well as core nodes. The seed that governs anything purchased during the pre-sale is unique and no future loot boxes will ever have as high of a chance of obtaining rare items! In addition, the pre-sale is one of the few times that core nodes are distributed to players. The majority of the core nodes in the game will be associated with titles, such as being the champion of a tournament, which can change. Core nodes obtained in the pre-sale are sent to your wallet as ERC-20 tokens and are fully owned by you.

Loot Box Opening

May 22, 2018

Shortly after the pre-sale is completed, pre-sale backers will have the opportunity to open their loot boxes and obtain their rewards. At this point, backers will find out whether they were lucky enough to obtain a core node or unique item. All assets will be sent to their wallet and viewable in their collection.

Marketplace Launch

June 7, 2018

Once pre-sale backers have had the opportunity to open their loot boxes, the marketplace will launch. This gives early backers the opportunity to trade their rewards and for those who missed out on the pre-sale a second chance to participate prior to the launch of the game. All players with an ethereum wallet can participate in the marketplace once it’s launched.

Minigame Rollout

June 30, 2018

Following the launch of the marketplace, minigames will be rolled out one by one. Any player with a presale item can participate in the beta testing of minigames. This will be an opportunity for early backers to provide input and shape the direction of the game. Players will get a sense of how the objects they own integrates into the minigames and changes the gameplay. At this stage, rewards from minigames are not permanently stored but early backers will get a chance to test strategies and sharpen their skills before launch.

Battle Rollout

July 10, 2018

Once a few of the minigames have been rolled out, the battle system will be integrated into the game. Pre-sale backers who own a Spark can participate in testing out the battle system. Like the minigame rollout, all results at this stage are not permanently stored but it gives early backers a way to develop strategies and provide feedback prior to the wider launch. During this process, additional minigames will continued to be rolled out.

Beta Launch

July 30, 2018

During the beta launch, pre-sale backers will be able to play on a test server and experience the full game as it is intended. Battle and minigame rewards will be permanently stored on that server so players will be able to get a sense of how the game economy will operate. Game features such as crafting will be implemented as well. The beta launch is intended to simulate the full game experience prior to the full launch. Anything won or created during this period will not carry over to the main server.

Game Launch

July 31, 2018

Once the game is fully launched, all players will have access to the game regardless of whether or not they own pre-sale items and begin earning loot boxes and game items. The launch event will feature limited time quests and giveaways. Competitive rankings and tournaments will be launched at this time as well and players will have the chance to earn core nodes.

Post-Launch

August 1, 2018

Following launch, the game will continue to be developed with input from players. New minigames and items will periodically be rolled out and the game world will continue to be expanded to include additional features. The community will play a vital role in determining how XXX grows and improves.
